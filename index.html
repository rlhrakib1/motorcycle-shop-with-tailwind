Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most important things I should know about riding a bike?

Give your bike a good once-over before hitting the road. Things you should check out every time you get on your motorcycle include tire pressure, mirrors and lights.

How can I tell if my helmet is old and I need a new one?

If you can see marks on the shell or measure any foam crush at all, replace the helmet.

My bike has been in storage is it safe to ride?

Probably not totally safe. It likely needs air and for various fittings, nuts and bolts to be checked.

What rules should I follow when riding my bike?

The most important rules to consider while riding a bike are:
1.Wear a Helmet
2.Proper riding gear
3.Don't be a squid. Ride safely and responsibly.
